Oral Health Basics

Healthy Habit Guides

Brushing Techniques

Brush at a 45-degree angle to your gums using short, circular strokes. Gentle brushing prevents gum recession and enamel wear.

Diet & Teeth Health

Incorporate calcium-rich foods (milk, cheese, yogurt) and fibrous vegetables into your diet. Fibrous foods scrub teeth clean naturally while chewing.

Cavity Red Flags

Persistent tooth sensitivity, bleeding gums while brushing, and bad breath are early signs of decay. Early detection saves roots and saves money!

Flossing & Gum Care

Floss daily to remove plaque from spaces between teeth where toothbrush bristles can't reach. Healthy gums shouldn't bleed; bleeding is a sign of early gingivitis.

Root Canal Truths

Modern RCT is comfortable and virtually painless. Preserving your natural tooth via root canal therapy maintains your original bite alignment and healthy jawbone density.

Kids' Care Timeline

Children should visit the dentist by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears. Early protective fluoride coatings help prevent cavities in deep molar grooves.
