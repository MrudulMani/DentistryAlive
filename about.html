About Our Clinic

Combining world-class clinical expertise with warmth, empathy, and high ethical standards.

Our Identity

Why we are "Dentistry Alive"

At Dentistry Alive, we believe that teeth are not just solid, inert bone structures. They are complex, living parts of your body. Our clinic name reflects our core philosophy: keeping your natural dentition active, strong, and healthy throughout your life.

We work tirelessly to preserve your natural teeth using conservative treatment techniques, avoiding unnecessary extractions, and using bio-compatible restorative materials that mimic natural tooth biology.

Our Core Pillars

  • 01
    Conservative Dentistry We focus heavily on tooth preservation, saving even heavily compromised teeth with expert Root Canals and crowns.
  • 02

  • 03

Safety & Integrity

Sterilization Protocols

Class-B Autoclaving

We use high-vacuum Class-B autoclaves to sterilize all dental instruments, killing 100% of bacterial spores, viruses, and pathogens.

Color Indicator Strips

Every sterilization pack contains chemical indicator strips that change color only when maximum temperature and pressure are successfully reached.

100% Disposables

Syringes, suction tips, patient drapes, tumbler glasses, and clinical gloves are strictly single-use and disposed of immediately after each session.

We take hygiene very seriously. Our water lines are flushed daily and treated with filtration pills, and all clinical countertops are wiped with hospital-grade disinfectant spray between appointments.
